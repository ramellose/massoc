<h2><em>massoc</em>,<br /> a platform for microbial associations.<br />
<em>massoc</em> can help you with:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Batch processing of BIOM and tab-delimited files</li>
<li>Preprocessing of files</li>
<li>Aggregation by taxonomic levels</li>
<li>Network inference from a single interface</li>
<li>Distribution of network inference jobs</li>
<li>More features are currently under construction.</li>
</ul>
